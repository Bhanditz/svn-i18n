Uhlobo Lokuthatha isampula yilo olusho ukuthi abanelayisensi banenkululeko engakanani. Eyokwenza isampula ivumela kuphela ukuba kusetshenziswe izingxenye ezithile futhi iyakwenqabela ukuba usetshenziswe wonke umsebenzi. Eyokwenza Isampula Nokunye, ivumela abanamalayisensi ukuba bathathe futhi bashintshe izingxenye ezithile zomsebenzi, futhi bahlanganyela wonke umsebenzi ngenjongo engeyona eyezentengiselwano. Eyokuthatha Isampula Nokunye ikala ukuthi abanelayisensi banenkululeko engakanani. Eyokuthatha Isampula ivumela kuphela izingxenye ukuba zisetshenziswe futhi ivimbela ukuhlanganyelwa kwawo wonke umsebenzi. Eyokuthatha Isampula Nokunye ivumela abanamalayisensi ukuba bathathe futhi baguqule umsebenzi, futhi bahlanganyele wonke umsebenzi ngezinjongo okungezona ezentengiselwano. Eyokuthatha Isampula Nokunye Okungekona Okwezentengiselwano iphinde ivimbele ilayisensi yokuthatha iSampula Nokunye ngokunikeza imvume kuphela ukuba kwakhiwe okungekona okwezentengiselwano kulo msebenzi.
