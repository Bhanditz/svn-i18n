Registri in gostovanje

Common Content je register in imenik del pod licencami Creative Commons.
Open Source Audio in Open Source Movies spletnega mesta Archive.org ponujata register in brezplačno gostovanje za avdiovizualna dela pod licencami Creative Commons.
